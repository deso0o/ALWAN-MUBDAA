Founder & Creative Director — Alwan Mobd3a
"I didn't start Alwan to make ads. I started it to make things that people can't look away from."

Ahmed El Desouki founded Alwan Mobd3a in 2014 with a vision that many thought was impossibly ambitious for a startup in Sharjah: to build a creative advertising studio that could compete — and win — at the government level.

His philosophy is rooted in an obsession with craft. Every sign, every printed piece, every vehicle wrap that leaves the Alwan workshop must pass one test: does it command attention? After a decade, that standard has never dropped — and it's what transformed a local studio into one of the UAE's most trusted advertising names.
